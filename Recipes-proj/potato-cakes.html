Old-Fashioned Potato Cakes

Description

Step back in time and savor the nostalgia of homemade goodness with our Old-Fashioned Potato Cakes. Crafted from tender potatoes, mashed to perfection and delicately seasoned with traditional spices, these golden-brown cakes offer a delightful crunch with each bite. Whether served as a comforting side dish or enjoyed as a satisfying snack, our potato cakes evoke the charm of yesteryear's kitchen creations. Crispy on the outside and fluffy on the inside, they are the perfect embodiment of simplicity and flavor. Rediscover the timeless appeal of homemade cooking with our Old-Fashioned Potato Cakes, a savory delight that brings warmth to every meal.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes: This recipe starts with two cups of prepared mashed potatoes. Start from scratch or use leftovers that would otherwise go to waste!
  • Flour: A cup of all-purpose flour gives the potato cakes structure and helps crisp them up nicely.
  • Onion: A diced onion lends flavor and texture.
  • Egg: An egg gives the potato cakes moisture and helps bind the batter together.
  • Seasonings: Season the potato cakes with salt and ground black pepper.
  • Oil: Fry the potato cakes in vegetable oil until they’re golden brown and crisp.

Steps

  1. Make the batter.
  2. Drop circles of batter into hot oil, then fry until they’re golden brown.
  3. Drain on paper towels.
