Old-Fashioned Vegetable Soup

Description

Indulge in the heartwarming flavors of the past with our Old-Fashioned Vegetable Soup. Bursting with wholesome goodness and comforting aromas, this timeless recipe combines a medley of fresh vegetables, hearty broth, and savory herbs. Each spoonful offers a taste of tradition, bringing back memories of cozy kitchens and cherished family gatherings. Made with care and attention to detail, our vegetable soup is a nourishing treat for the body and soul. Whether enjoyed as a light lunch or a soothing dinner, it's a classic dish that never goes out of style. So sit back, savor the comforting flavors, and let our Old-Fashioned Vegetable Soup transport you to a simpler time.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 large carrots, diced
  • 3 stalks celery, diced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can whole peeled tomatoes, chopped, juice reserved
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Â½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 2 quarts beef broth

Steps

  1. In a large pot over medium heat, melt butter. Cook onion, carrots and celery until onion is translucent. Stir in tomatoes with their juice, salt, pepper, parsley, soy sauce, Worcestershire and paprika. Pour in beef broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer 30 minutes, until vegetables are tender and flavors are well blended.
